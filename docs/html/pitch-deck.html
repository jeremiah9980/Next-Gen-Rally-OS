Public Site

Scheduled background polling of NCS team pages. Diffs live data against stored snapshots and queues every change for coach review. Never auto-applies.

Key Features

Built around
how coaches actually work.

Live NCS Team Search

Search playncs.com by name, city, or state, pick your team, and pull the live roster in seconds — no copy-paste required.

Change Governance Queue

Every detected NCS roster or tournament change is queued for explicit coach approval. Nothing changes without human sign-off.

AI Practice Planning

Claude generates age-group-appropriate, standard-aligned practice sessions with drills, timing, and coaching points.

Order-Independent Parser

Paste any NCS roster table — header or positional — and the parser extracts jersey, name, position, grad year, bats, throws automatically.

Org Kit Generator

Provisioning a team generates a starter brand kit: palette, SVG logo with team initials, social post templates, and a pre-filled Vercel checklist.

13-Module Public Site

Home, roster, schedule, tournaments, practice plans, player profiles, GameChanger stats, fundraising, and more — all config-driven.
